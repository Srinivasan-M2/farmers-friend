Know your soil and choose the best crop for it

1. Alluvial Soil

Color: Light Grey to Ash Grey

Properties: Rich in potash, ideal water retention

Suitable Crops: Rice, Wheat, Sugarcane, Pulses

2. Black Soil

Color: Deep Black to Grey

Properties: Moisture-retaining, rich in lime, iron, magnesium

Suitable Crops: Cotton, Groundnut, Soybean, Sorghum

3. Red Soil

Color: Reddish due to iron content

Properties: Poor in nitrogen, phosphorus

Suitable Crops: Millets, Pulses, Potatoes, Groundnut

4. Laterite Soil

Color: Reddish-brown

Properties: Poor fertility, needs fertilizers

Suitable Crops: Tea, Coffee, Cashew, Coconut

5. Desert Soil

Color: Sandy Yellow or Red

Properties: Dry, lacks organic matter

Suitable Crops: Bajra, Guar, Barley, Dates

6. Mountain Soil

Color: Varies by region

Properties: Rich in humus, coarse texture

Suitable Crops: Tea, Coffee, Fruits, Spices
